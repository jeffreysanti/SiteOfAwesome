Public Viewing

By default your profile will be only visible to anyone you create an account for. You can enable public viewing here. When you do so, the public will only access the home page with no content visible to them. For your privacy only information explictly given permission for the group "public" will be visible to them.


Groups

Groups are SiteOfAwesomes's way of organizing what content is visible to specific clients. While, you certainly are able to control what an individual client can or cannot see, groups make it much easier to manage categories of clients. Note that a particular client's access overrides the group access.
